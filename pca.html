主成分分析 (PCA)

將數千個基因維度壓縮成數十個主成分——保留關鍵變異、抑制噪音、為下游分群與視覺化奠基。

Compress thousands of gene dimensions into tens of principal components — retain key variation, suppress noise, and lay the groundwork for clustering and visualization.

概覽

一、為什麼 scRNA-seq 需要 PCA？

單細胞數據動輒包含 20,000+ 基因，即使做了 HVG 篩選後仍剩約 2,000 維特徵。直接在這個高維空間做聚類、UMAP，會遇到所謂「維度災難 (Curse of Dimensionality)」——當維度過高時：

  • 細胞間的歐氏距離趨於同質化，鄰居關係失去鑑別力
  • 下游算法計算量隨維度線性甚至指數增長
  • 噪音（drop-out、技術變異）會掩蓋真實生物訊號

PCA 的核心想法：在所有可能的線性方向中，找出資料變異量最大的那幾個軸（主成分），用它們來重新表達細胞。前幾個 PC 通常就足以捕捉細胞之間的主要差異（細胞類型、狀態），而後面的 PC 多半是技術噪音——直接丟棄。

Single-cell datasets routinely contain 20,000+ genes; even after HVG selection ~2,000 features remain. Clustering or UMAP directly in this high-dimensional space hits the so-called "Curse of Dimensionality":

  • Euclidean distances between cells become homogeneous — neighborhood structure loses discriminative power
  • Downstream algorithms scale linearly or worse in dimensionality
  • Noise (dropout, technical variation) drowns out real biological signal

The core idea of PCA: among all possible linear directions, find those that capture maximal data variance (principal components) and use them to re-represent cells. The first few PCs typically suffice to capture major cell-to-cell differences (cell types, states); later PCs are mostly technical noise — discard them.

🗜️

降維

從 ~2000 維壓縮到 10–50 維，保留 70–90% 的有效變異。

Compress ~2000 dims to 10–50, retaining 70–90% of meaningful variance.

🧹

去噪

低方差 PC 多半是雜訊；丟棄它們等同於做頻譜過濾。

Low-variance PCs are mostly noise; discarding them is equivalent to spectral filtering.

⚡

加速

後續 kNN、UMAP、Leiden 都在 PCA 空間中運作，速度提升一個數量級。

Downstream kNN, UMAP, Leiden all operate in PCA space — an order of magnitude faster.

💡
關鍵原則：PCA 在 scRNA-seq 中不是用來做視覺化（那是 UMAP 的工作），而是下游分析的入口空間——所有 kNN、聚類、UMAP 投影都在這個 PCA 空間中進行。因此 PCA 的品質直接決定整個下游結果。 Key principle: PCA in scRNA-seq is not for visualization (that's UMAP's job) — it is the entry space for downstream analysis. All kNN graphs, clustering, and UMAP projection happen in PCA space. The quality of PCA therefore directly determines the quality of everything downstream.
數學直覺

二、PCA 背後的數學直覺

從幾何角度看，PCA 在做這件事：

  1. 中心化資料：每個基因減去平均（這就是為什麼 Scaling 步驟必要）
  2. 計算共變異矩陣 Σ = (1/n) XᵀX（基因 × 基因的矩陣）
  3. 特徵分解：Σ = V Λ Vᵀ。特徵向量 V 就是主成分方向，特徵值 Λ 是該方向上的變異量
  4. 投影：原始細胞 × 基因矩陣 X 投影到前 k 個特徵向量，得到細胞 × k 的降維表現 X·V[:,1:k]

SVD 視角：實務上幾乎不會真的算共變異矩陣，而是用 SVD 分解 X = U Σ Vᵀ。U·Σ 就是細胞在 PC 空間的座標，Vᵀ 是基因 loadings。

為什麼 scRNA-seq 用 randomized / truncated SVD？因為我們只要前 50 個 PC，不需要完整分解。Seurat 的 irlba、Scanpy 的 arpack 都是這種「近似但夠用」的演算法，把 O(n³) 的全分解降到 O(n²·k)，對 10⁵–10⁶ 個細胞至關重要。

Geometrically, PCA does the following:

  1. Center the data: subtract per-gene mean (this is why Scaling matters)
  2. Compute the covariance matrix Σ = (1/n) XᵀX (a gene × gene matrix)
  3. Eigendecomposition: Σ = V Λ Vᵀ. Eigenvectors V are PC directions; eigenvalues Λ are variances along those directions
  4. Projection: project cell × gene matrix X onto the top k eigenvectors → cell × k embedding X·V[:,1:k]

SVD viewpoint: in practice we almost never compute the covariance matrix directly. Instead we use SVD X = U Σ Vᵀ. U·Σ gives PC-space coordinates, Vᵀ are gene loadings.

Why scRNA-seq uses randomized / truncated SVD: we only need the top 50 PCs, not the full decomposition. Seurat's irlba and Scanpy's arpack are such "approximate but sufficient" methods, reducing O(n³) full decomposition to O(n²·k) — critical for 10⁵–10⁶ cells.

🎯

變異量分解

每個 PC 解釋固定比例的總方差。前 10–30 個 PC 通常累積 60–90% 解釋率，其餘多為噪音。累積解釋率曲線是選擇 npcs 的重要依據。

Each PC explains a fixed fraction of total variance. The first 10–30 PCs typically cumulate 60–90% explained variance; the rest are mostly noise. The cumulative explained variance curve is a key reference for choosing npcs.

🧭

PC 載荷

每個 PC 都是基因的線性組合；高 loading 的基因驅動該 PC 的方向。檢視 top loadings 可以判斷這個 PC 是反映「細胞類型」、「細胞週期」還是「批次效應」。

Each PC is a linear combination of genes; high-loading genes drive its direction. Inspecting top loadings tells you whether a PC reflects "cell type," "cell cycle," or "batch effects."

決定 nPCs

三、如何決定要用幾個主成分？

「要用幾個 PC？」是 PCA 步驟唯一需要人工決策的參數。常見三種方法：

"How many PCs?" is the only manual decision in the PCA step. Three common approaches:

📈

① Elbow Plot

畫出每個 PC 解釋的方差比例（標準差），找曲線「手肘」處——下降開始趨平的位置。直觀快速，但「手肘」位置主觀。

Plot variance (or std-dev) explained per PC; find the "elbow" where the curve flattens. Intuitive and fast, but the elbow position is subjective.

🧪

② JackStraw 檢定

隨機打亂 1% 基因表現後重算 PCA，計算每個 PC 的 p-value——統計上顯著的 PC 才保留。準確但極慢（重複百次）。

Permute 1% of gene expression, re-run PCA, compute a p-value for each PC — keep statistically significant ones. Accurate but very slow (hundreds of permutations).

📊

③ 累積解釋率

累積解釋率達 80–90% 時截斷。客觀但對 scRNA-seq 可能取過多 PC（因為 single-cell 噪音多）。

Truncate when cumulative explained variance reaches 80–90%. Objective, but may overshoot for scRNA-seq (single-cell noise inflates tails).

🌳 實務建議

經驗值:
大多數 PBMC / 組織數據集 15–30 個 PC 就足夠；複雜異質組織（多細胞類型 + 多狀態）可以選 30–50。
原則:
寧可多選一點。多選 5 個 PC 帶來的噪音遠小於少選 5 個遺失的稀有族群訊號。
驗證:
用 DimHeatmap() 檢視每個 PC 的 top loading 基因；如果 PC 30 還有清晰的生物學意義基因群，就再多取幾個。
迭代:
npcs 不是一錘定音——可以先選 20 跑分群，看結果合理性，必要時調整重跑。
Rule of thumb:
Most PBMC / tissue datasets need 15–30 PCs; complex heterogeneous tissues (many types × states) may need 30–50.
Principle:
Lean toward more PCs. Noise from 5 extra PCs is much less harmful than losing rare-population signal from 5 too few.
Validate:
Use DimHeatmap() to inspect top-loading genes per PC. If PC 30 still shows clear biological gene programs, take a few more.
Iterate:
npcs is not set in stone — pick 20, run clustering, judge biological plausibility, adjust if needed.
⚠️
太少 PC 的風險：會遺失稀有細胞群的訊號（它們的變異可能集中在 PC 20–30 之間）。太多 PC 的風險：引入噪音，使 kNN 圖出現虛假連結，UMAP 上會看到雜亂的細點。經驗上「太少 PC」傷害更大。 Risk of too few PCs: losing rare population signal (their variance may sit at PCs 20–30). Risk of too many PCs: noise creates spurious edges in the kNN graph and scattered points on UMAP. Empirically, "too few" is the worse failure mode.

最佳實務

七、最佳實務 Checklist

✅ 先 scale 再 PCA

每個基因的方差差異極大；不 scale 就會被 housekeeping / ribosomal 基因主導。Seurat ScaleData()、Scanpy sc.pp.scale() 都會做 z-score。

Variance varies enormously between genes; without scaling, housekeeping / ribosomal genes dominate. Both Seurat ScaleData() and Scanpy sc.pp.scale() apply z-scoring.

✅ 先在 HVG 上做

只取 ~2000 個 HVG 跑 PCA，加速 10×，且結果通常更乾淨——大部分低變異基因只是噪音。

Run PCA on just ~2000 HVGs — 10× faster, and usually cleaner; most low-variance genes are just noise.

✅ 檢視 PC1 / PC2 loadings

用 VizDimLoadings 或 sc.pl.pca_loadings。如果 PC1 全是 cell cycle 基因，代表細胞週期主導變異——需要回去 regress out。

Use VizDimLoadings or sc.pl.pca_loadings. If PC1 is dominated by cell-cycle genes, the cell cycle is driving variance — go back and regress it out.

✅ 固定 random seed

Randomized SVD 是隨機算法——不同 seed 得到的 PC 方向可能正負相反。Seurat 預設 seed=42；Scanpy 用 random_state 參數。

Randomized SVD is stochastic — different seeds can flip PC signs. Seurat defaults to seed=42; Scanpy uses random_state.

✅ 下游分析才用

PC1×PC2 散佈圖很少能展示完整生物結構——只用於除錯（看是否有批次主導 PC）。展示細胞結構請用 UMAP。

PC1×PC2 scatter rarely shows full biological structure — use it only for debugging (checking whether a batch dominates a PC). For showing cell structure, use UMAP.

✅ 多樣本先整合

多批次資料若直接 PCA，PC1 通常會是「批次」。建議先用 Harmony / RPCA / scVI 整合再跑 PCA（或直接用整合後的 embedding 取代 PCA）。

For multi-batch data, naive PCA often gives "batch" as PC1. Run Harmony / RPCA / scVI integration first (or use the integrated embedding in place of PCA).

常見陷阱

八、常見陷阱

🚫 未 scale 就 PCA

症狀：PC1 解釋率異常高（>40%），所有 PC1 高 loading 基因是 ribosomal / mitochondrial。
原因：高表現基因的絕對方差自然大，主導 PC1。
解法：重做 ScaleData 或 sc.pp.scale。

Symptom: PC1 explained variance abnormally high (>40%); top PC1 loading genes are ribosomal / mitochondrial.
Cause: high-expression genes naturally have large absolute variance and dominate PC1.
Fix: rerun ScaleData or sc.pp.scale.

🚫 細胞週期主導 PC1

症狀：PC1 loadings 是 MKI67、PCNA、TOP2A 等週期基因；UMAP 上同類型細胞被分成 G1/S/G2M 三群。
解法：計算 cell cycle score 後在 ScaleData(vars.to.regress) 中扣除，或使用 SCTransform 的 vars.to.regress 參數。

Symptom: PC1 loadings are cycle genes (MKI67, PCNA, TOP2A); same cell type splits into G1/S/G2M groups on UMAP.
Fix: compute cell cycle scores and regress them out via ScaleData(vars.to.regress), or use the vars.to.regress argument in SCTransform.

🚫 批次效應主導

症狀：PC1 或 PC2 完美區分樣本來源而非細胞類型。
解法：聚類前用 Harmony / RPCA / scVI 進行 integration，得到批次校正後的 embedding 後再做後續 kNN / UMAP。

Symptom: PC1 or PC2 perfectly separates sample identity rather than cell type.
Fix: integrate (Harmony / RPCA / scVI) before clustering; use the batch-corrected embedding for downstream kNN / UMAP.

🚫 nPCs 太少

症狀：稀有細胞群在 UMAP 上「不見了」或被併入相鄰大群。
解法：把 npcs 從 10 提高到 30；用 DimHeatmap 確認 PC 20–30 是否仍有生物意義基因群。

Symptom: rare populations "disappear" on UMAP or merge into adjacent clusters.
Fix: raise npcs from 10 to 30; use DimHeatmap to verify PCs 20–30 still carry biologically meaningful gene programs.

🚫 SCT 流程的 PC 不同

症狀：用 SCTransform 後 PC loadings 看起來「不太一樣」。
說明：SCT 用 Pearson residuals 取代 z-score，數值意義不同——loadings 應理解為「殘差空間的方向」。npcs 選擇方法不變，但解讀 loadings 要小心。

Symptom: after SCTransform, PC loadings look "different."
Note: SCT uses Pearson residuals in place of z-scores — loadings live in residual space. The npcs selection method is unchanged, but interpret loadings with this in mind.

🚫 raw counts 直接做 PCA

症狀：PC1 解釋率 >60%，且高度與 nCount_RNA 相關。
原因：未做 log-normalize，total counts 變成最大的方差來源。
解法：必須先 NormalizeData / sc.pp.normalize_total + log1p。

Symptom: PC1 explained variance >60% and highly correlated with nCount_RNA.
Cause: no log-normalization — total counts become the largest variance source.
Fix: always run NormalizeData / sc.pp.normalize_total + log1p first.
