ADVANCED

細胞通訊 (Cell-Cell Communication)

透過 ligand-receptor 推論細胞間訊號傳遞，繪製組織微環境的「對話地圖」。

Infer intercellular signaling from ligand-receptor expression to map the "conversation network" of a tissue microenvironment.

概覽

一、為什麼研究細胞間通訊？

多細胞生物並非「細胞各做各的」——組織恆定 (tissue homeostasis)、發育、免疫反應與腫瘤微環境，全部依賴細胞之間綿密的訊號交換。最常見的通訊機制是分泌型分子（如 cytokine、growth factor）與細胞表面受體結合，觸發下游 signaling cascade。

scRNA-seq 同時測量所有細胞的 transcriptome，提供史無前例的機會：從一張快照推論「誰在跟誰講話」。代表工具有 CellChat (Jin et al., Nat Commun 2021)、CellPhoneDB (Efremova 2020)、NicheNet (Browaeys 2020)、LIANA (Dimitrov 2022) 等。本章以 CellChat 為主軸，並比較各工具差異。

Multicellular organisms are not "each cell doing its own thing" — tissue homeostasis, development, immune responses, and tumor microenvironments all rely on dense networks of intercellular signaling. The most common mechanism is secreted molecules (cytokines, growth factors) binding to surface receptors, triggering downstream signaling cascades.

scRNA-seq simultaneously measures every cell's transcriptome, offering unprecedented opportunity: inferring "who is talking to whom" from a single snapshot. Major tools include CellChat (Jin et al., Nat Commun 2021), CellPhoneDB (Efremova 2020), NicheNet (Browaeys 2020), and LIANA (Dimitrov 2022). This chapter centers on CellChat with comparisons across tools.

🧬

發育生物學

胚胎發育依賴 Wnt、BMP、Notch、SHH 等 morphogen 梯度。L-R 分析能還原組織發育中誘導訊號的方向與強度。

Embryonic development relies on Wnt, BMP, Notch, SHH morphogen gradients. L-R analysis reconstructs the direction and strength of inductive signaling.

🛡️

免疫微環境

T 細胞、B 細胞、樹突細胞、巨噬細胞透過 cytokine、checkpoint 分子 (PD-L1/PD-1) 互動。L-R 分析揭示免疫抑制或活化網絡。

T cells, B cells, DCs, and macrophages interact via cytokines and checkpoint molecules (PD-L1/PD-1). L-R analysis reveals immunosuppressive or activating networks.

🧪

腫瘤微環境

癌細胞、CAF、TAM、T 細胞之間的 cross-talk 決定腫瘤進程與免疫逃脫。CellChat 是分析 TME 的常用利器。

Cross-talk among cancer cells, CAFs, TAMs, and T cells dictates tumor progression and immune escape. CellChat is a standard tool for TME analysis.

💡
核心原則：L-R 分析是「推論」而非「測量」。scRNA-seq 只能觀察到 mRNA 是否存在，無法保證蛋白質真的被翻譯、修飾、運送到細胞表面、與 receptor 形成功能性結合。L-R hit 是 hypothesis-generating，不是 ground truth。 Core principle: L-R analysis is inference, not measurement. scRNA-seq only sees whether mRNA exists — it cannot confirm that the protein is translated, modified, surface-localized, or engaged in a functional binding. An L-R hit is hypothesis-generating, not ground truth.

💨 分泌型訊號

細胞分泌可擴散的分子，如 cytokines、chemokines、growth factors，可作用於鄰近或遠端細胞（paracrine / endocrine）。

例：TGF-β、IL-6、VEGF、TNF。

Cells secrete diffusible molecules (cytokines, chemokines, growth factors) acting on nearby or distant cells (paracrine/endocrine).

e.g., TGF-β, IL-6, VEGF, TNF.

🤝 細胞接觸型

需要直接物理接觸，受體與配體都嵌在細胞膜上。

例：Notch-DLL/JAG、PD-L1/PD-1、CD40L/CD40。

Requires direct physical contact — ligand and receptor are both membrane-anchored.

e.g., Notch-DLL/JAG, PD-L1/PD-1, CD40L/CD40.

🕸️ ECM-Receptor

細胞外基質 (collagen、laminin、fibronectin) 與細胞表面 integrin、CD44 等結合，傳遞機械與位置資訊。

Extracellular matrix (collagen, laminin, fibronectin) binds surface integrins, CD44 etc., relaying mechanical and positional cues.

🔄 非分類其他

包含代謝物（如 lipids、amino acids）作為訊號的互動，部分新版資料庫已納入（CellChat v2 / NeuronChat）。

Includes metabolites (lipids, amino acids) as signaling molecules — partially included in newer DB versions (CellChat v2, NeuronChat).

資料庫進一步把 L-R 互動分組成 signaling pathway，例如 TGF-β pathway 同時包含 TGFB1→TGFBR1/TGFBR2、TGFB2→… 等多對 L-R；分析時除了單一 L-R，更常用路徑層級（pathway-level）的訊號強度與分布，因為單一 L-R 容易受 dropout 影響，路徑層級結果更穩定也更具生物學意義。CellChat 預設提供 TGF-β、Notch、Wnt、BMP、CCL、CXCL、PD-L1、HLA、COLLAGEN、LAMININ 等數十個 pathway。

The DB further groups interactions into signaling pathways: e.g., the TGF-β pathway contains TGFB1→TGFBR1/TGFBR2, TGFB2→…, etc. Beyond individual L-R, pathway-level signaling is usually more robust because single L-R pairs suffer from dropout. CellChat ships with dozens of preset pathways — TGF-β, Notch, Wnt, BMP, CCL, CXCL, PD-L1, HLA, COLLAGEN, LAMININ and many more.

計算邏輯

三、CellChat 如何計算通訊機率？

CellChat 採用質量作用定律 (Law of Mass Action) 對 L-R interaction 建模。對於某一 L-R 對在「來源細胞群 (source) → 目標細胞群 (target)」之間的通訊機率 P，本質上是：

P (source → target) = f(L̄_source) × f(R̄_target) × (co-factor 修正)

  • L̄_source：source group 中 ligand 的平均表達（Hill 函數轉換為飽和效應）。
  • R̄_target：target group 中 receptor 的平均表達。多亞基複合體取幾何平均。
  • Co-factor 修正：考慮 agonists / antagonists 對配對交互的增強或抑制。
  • 排列檢定 (permutation test)：將細胞標籤打散 n=100 次，計算實際 P 大於隨機 P 的次數，得到 p-value。

核心直覺：兩群細胞要被認定有訊號連結，ligand 必須真的在 source 中表達、receptor 必須真的在 target 中表達——只有單邊高表達不算 hit。Permutation 確保 hit 不是因為某個 group 細胞數很多、表達看起來就會偏高的偽影。

CellChat models L-R interactions using the law of mass action. The communication probability P from a source group to a target group, for a given L-R pair, is essentially:

P (source → target) = f(L̄_source) × f(R̄_target) × (co-factor adjustment)

  • L̄_source: mean ligand expression in the source group (passed through a Hill function for saturation).
  • R̄_target: mean receptor expression in the target group. Multi-subunit complexes use geometric mean.
  • Co-factor adjustment: incorporates agonists/antagonists that enhance or suppress the interaction.
  • Permutation test: shuffle cell labels n=100 times; count how often the observed P exceeds permuted P → p-value.

Intuition: for two groups to be considered communicating, the ligand must actually be expressed in source AND the receptor in target — one side alone does not score. Permutation guards against the artifact where large groups simply look highly expressed.

小群細胞 = 雜訊放大器：觀察滑桿——當「每群細胞數」拉到 20 以下，permutation null 變得非常寬，p-value 即使在 L̄、R̄ 都中等時也跳得很厲害。實務上 n < 10 的細胞群通訊推論幾乎不可信。若有此類群，請合併或從分析中排除。 Small groups amplify noise: push "cells per group" below ~20 and the null distribution widens dramatically — p-values become unstable even at moderate L̄ and R̄. In practice, communication inference is unreliable for groups with n < 10. Merge or exclude such groups.
程式碼

六、實作範例

# 1. 建立 CellChat 物件 / Create object
library(CellChat)
# 重要：使用「正規化但未 scale」的 counts（CellChat 內部會 scale）
# Important: pass normalized (NOT scaled) data — CellChat scales internally
data.input <- GetAssayData(pbmc, assay = "RNA", slot = "data")
meta <- pbmc@meta.data
cellchat <- createCellChat(object = data.input, meta = meta, group.by = "cell_type")

# 2. 載入資料庫並過濾 / Load DB and filter
cellchat@DB <- CellChatDB.human
cellchat <- subsetData(cellchat)
cellchat <- identifyOverExpressedGenes(cellchat)
cellchat <- identifyOverExpressedInteractions(cellchat)

# 3. 計算通訊機率（核心步驟） / Core step: compute probabilities
cellchat <- computeCommunProb(cellchat, type = "triMean", nboot = 100)
cellchat <- filterCommunication(cellchat, min.cells = 10)
cellchat <- computeCommunProbPathway(cellchat)
cellchat <- aggregateNet(cellchat)

# 4. 視覺化 / Visualize
netVisual_circle(cellchat@net$weight, weight.scale = TRUE)
netVisual_bubble(cellchat, sources.use = 1, targets.use = c(2,3,4))
netVisual_aggregate(cellchat, signaling = "TGFb", layout = "chord")

# 5. 中心性分析（誰是 hub？） / Centrality: who is a hub?
cellchat <- netAnalysis_computeCentrality(cellchat, slot.name = "netP")
netAnalysis_signalingRole_scatter(cellchat)  # sender vs receiver score
# CellPhoneDB v5 — Python statistical method
from cellphonedb.src.core.methods import cpdb_statistical_analysis_method

results = cpdb_statistical_analysis_method.call(
    cpdb_file_path     = "cellphonedb.zip",
    meta_file_path     = "meta.tsv",           # cell_id, cell_type 兩欄
    counts_file_path   = "counts.h5ad",        # normalized counts
    counts_data        = "hgnc_symbol",
    iterations         = 1000,
    threshold          = 0.1,                 # 10% 細胞表達門檻 / 10% expression threshold
    threads            = 8,
    output_path        = "./out"
)
# 輸出：significant_means.txt + pvalues.txt + deconvoluted.txt
# 用 ktplotspy 或自己畫 dot plot
import ktplotspy as kp
kp.plot_cpdb(adata=adata, cell_type1="T", cell_type2="Mono",
             means="out/means.txt", pvals="out/pvalues.txt")

import liana as li
import scanpy as sc

# 跑單一方法

                  expr_prop=0.1, verbose=True)

# 視覺化（dot plot）

進階分析

七、進階分析

🔁 跨條件差異訊號

真正有生物學意義的問題往往是「對照組與治療組的訊號網絡有什麼不同？」CellChat 提供 mergeCellChat() 與 compareInteractions()，回答兩種尺度：

  • 數量差異 (number of interactions)：哪些 cell pair 在疾病組多了 / 少了訊號。
  • 強度差異 (interaction strength)：相同 L-R 在兩條件下的 P 倍率。
  • 新興 / 喪失 pathway：rankNet() 對 pathway 排序，找出在某條件下顯著上升的訊號。

The biologically meaningful question is often "How does the signaling network differ between control and disease?" CellChat answers this with mergeCellChat() and compareInteractions() at two scales:

  • Number of interactions: which cell pairs gain/lose signaling.
  • Interaction strength: P fold-change of the same L-R between conditions.
  • Emerging/lost pathways: rankNet() ranks pathways to find condition-specific signaling.

🌐 訊號角色與中心性

CellChat 將每個細胞群在每個 pathway 中的角色拆解為 sender、receiver、mediator、influencer 四個 graph-centrality 分數：

  • Sender (outgoing)：對外發出訊號的能力。
  • Receiver (incoming)：對外來訊號的反應能力。
  • Mediator：是否在網絡中扮演「中介橋樑」。
  • Influencer：對網絡訊息流動的整體影響。

用 netAnalysis_signalingRole_scatter() 把 sender vs receiver 畫成散佈圖，可一眼識別「主要訊號源」與「主要訊號靶」。

CellChat decomposes each cell group's role in each pathway into four graph-centrality scores: sender, receiver, mediator, influencer:

  • Sender (outgoing): ability to emit signal.
  • Receiver (incoming): ability to respond to incoming signal.
  • Mediator: bridging role in the network.
  • Influencer: overall impact on information flow.

netAnalysis_signalingRole_scatter() plots sender vs receiver — at a glance you can spot the "major source" and "major sink" of any pathway.

最佳實務

八、最佳實務與常見陷阱

✅ 最佳實務清單

①
使用正規化但未 scale 的表達矩陣。CellChat 的 Hill 函數預設 normalized 為輸入，給 raw counts 或 z-score 都會產生錯誤結果。
②
跨條件比較務必統一細胞群標籤。如果對照組叫「CD4_naive」、疾病組叫「naive_CD4」，CellChat 會視為不同細胞群。
③
解讀以 pathway-level 為主，再下鑽到單一 L-R。單一 L-R 受 dropout 影響大；pathway 層級綜合多個冗餘的 L-R，結果穩定許多。
④
有 spatial transcriptomics 數據時交叉驗證——空間鄰近的 sender-receiver pair 才是真正可能發生 paracrine 的 hit。
⑤
論文 / 報告中明確標示：CellChatDB 版本、nboot 次數、p-value cutoff、是否做了 condition harmonization。
①
Use normalized (not scaled) expression. CellChat's Hill function expects normalized input — passing raw counts or z-scores gives wrong results.
②
Harmonize cell-type labels across conditions. If control has "CD4_naive" and disease has "naive_CD4", CellChat treats them as distinct groups.
③
Interpret at pathway-level first, then drill down. Single L-R pairs are noisy under dropout; pathways aggregate redundant pairs and are far more robust.
④
If spatial transcriptomics is available, cross-validate — only spatially adjacent sender-receiver pairs can plausibly engage in paracrine signaling.
⑤
In manuscripts/reports, explicitly state: CellChatDB version, nboot, p-value cutoff, condition harmonization steps.

🚧 常見陷阱 Top 5

  1. 方向搞反 (source vs target)：CellChat 規定 ligand 在 source、receptor 在 target。畫 chord diagram 時 sources/targets 對調就會誤判生物學方向。
  2. 用小細胞群推論：n < 10 的群 permutation null 太寬，hit 多半是 false positive；先合併或排除。
  3. Batch effect 散播到 L-R：未做 integration 的多樣本，某 ligand 在某 batch 特別高就被誤推成 hit。建議先做 batch correction 再跑 CellChat。
  4. 把 in vitro 結果套到 in vivo：器官切片中的 paracrine 需要空間鄰近；2D 培養細胞無空間限制。in vitro hit ≠ in vivo hit。
  5. Autocrine 主導畫面：許多 cell type 同時表達 ligand + receptor，autocrine score 常常蓋過 paracrine。如果只關心 cross-talk，請過濾 source == target。
  1. Reversed direction (source vs target): CellChat assumes ligand=source, receptor=target. Swapping sources/targets in chord diagrams flips biological direction.
  2. Inferring on small groups: with n < 10 the null is wide, hits are mostly false positives. Merge or exclude.
  3. Batch effects bleeding into L-R: without integration, a ligand inflated in one batch becomes a spurious hit. Run batch correction first.
  4. Generalizing in vitro to in vivo: paracrine in tissue requires spatial proximity; 2D culture has none. in vitro hit ≠ in vivo hit.
  5. Autocrine dominates the view: many cell types express both L and R, so autocrine scores often overshadow paracrine. Filter source ≠ target if cross-talk is the focus.
